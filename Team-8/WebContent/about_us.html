ABOUT US:

Shop It! Pvt Ltd. is an Indian electronic commerce company based in Bengaluru, India. Founded by Uday Mukherjee and Sameer Dutta(senior-junior at Indian Institute of Technology Delhi & colleagues at XYZ) in 2018, the company initially focused on book sales, before expanding into other product categories such as consumer electronics, fashion, and lifestyle products. The service competes primarily with Shop It's Indian subsidiary, and the domestic rival Snapdeal,Amazon. As of 2017, Flipkart held a 39.5% market share of India's e-commerce industry. Flipkart is significantly dominant in the sale of apparel (a position that was bolstered by its acquisitions of Myntra and Jabong.com), and was described as being "neck and neck" with Amazon in the sale of electronics and mobile phones.Shop It! also owns PhonePay, a mobile payments service based on the Unified Payments Interface (UPI).
